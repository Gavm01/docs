## Connect Your GHL Account

If you use GoHighLevel (GHL) for your business, Clara can sync conversations and contacts from GHL directly into your AgentClara inbox.

This means:
- **One inbox for everything** (GHL conversations + SMS + Email + Social)
- **Contacts sync** from GHL to AgentClara
- **Appointments sync** between both systems
- **No duplicate work** (don't manage conversations in two places)

## Set Up GHL Integration

<Steps>

<Step title="Open Integrations">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)**
</Step>

<Step title="Find GHL">
Look for **"GoHighLevel"** and click **Connect**
</Step>

<Step title="Log Into Your GHL Account">
You'll be redirected to GHL. Log in with your GHL username and password.
</Step>

<Step title="Grant Permissions">
GHL will ask if AgentClara can:
- ✅ Read your contacts
- ✅ Read your conversations
- ✅ Read your opportunities
- ✅ Sync appointments

Click **Approve** or **Allow**.
</Step>

<Step title="Back to AgentClara">
You'll be redirected back to AgentClara. It will say **"Connected"**.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Sync">
GHL conversations and contacts will start appearing in your AgentClara inbox. This takes 5-10 minutes.

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)** and you'll see GHL conversations labeled as "GHL" channel.
</Step>

</Steps>

## View GHL Conversations

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)**

Filter by **Channel: GHL** to see only GHL conversations.

Each conversation shows:
- **Customer name**
- **Last message** (what they said)
- **Contact info** from GHL (phone, email)
- **Opportunities** linked to them

Click any conversation to see the full thread.

## Respond to GHL Messages

Click a GHL conversation and type your reply. It will:
1. Send through AgentClara
2. Appear in GHL automatically
3. Get sent to the customer

No need to log into GHL separately.

## How Contacts Sync

Your GHL contacts automatically appear in AgentClara:

**When viewing a conversation**, you'll see:
- **Customer name**
- **Phone number**
- **Email**
- **Associated opportunities**
- **Linked deals**

If you create a new contact in GHL, they'll appear in AgentClara within 24 hours.

## Appointments & Calendar

If your GHL account uses Calendly or has appointment bookings:

1. Those appointments appear in AgentClara's calendar view
2. Clara can see your GHL availability
3. When a customer books through Clara, it syncs back to GHL
4. Both systems stay in sync

**No double-booking problems.**

## Managing Your Integration

### Disconnect GHL

If you want to stop syncing:

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)** > **GoHighLevel** > **Disconnect**

**Note:** Past conversations stay in AgentClara, but new GHL messages won't sync.

### Re-authorize

If GHL permissions change, reconnect:

1. Click **Disconnect**
2. Click **Connect** again
3. Log into GHL and approve permissions

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I connected GHL but don't see conversations">
  1. Sync can take 5-15 minutes. Wait and refresh the page.
  2. Do you have conversations in GHL? (Check your GHL account)
  3. Are they in a funnel that has messaging enabled?

  If still not working, email support@agentclara.ai
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="A contact isn't syncing from GHL">
  1. Refresh the page (data might not have loaded)
  2. Create a NEW contact in GHL and try again
  3. Contact might have an issue in GHL (missing phone/email)

  Contacts with incomplete info sometimes fail to sync.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I replied to a GHL message but it didn't show in GHL">
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh both AgentClara and GHL
  3. The message might be pending (check timestamp)

  If messages keep failing, disconnect and reconnect the integration.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can Clara respond to GHL messages automatically?">
  Yes! Clara's responses will automatically:
  1. Go to the GHL contact
  2. Appear in their GHL conversation
  3. Show as coming from your business

  No special setup needed—it just works.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need separate Clara knowledge for GHL?">
  No. Use the same knowledge base for all channels. But you can set a note in Clara's system prompt about GHL-specific responses if needed.
</Accordion>
