## Outbound Calling at Scale

Instead of manually calling customers one by one, let Clara do it. She can:
- **Call hundreds or thousands of customers** in minutes
- **Deliver consistent messaging** (every call has the same quality)
- **Book appointments** directly from the call
- **Handle objections** intelligently
- **Log results automatically** (no manual data entry)

## How It Works

1. **You upload a list** - CSV file with phone numbers (or we can pull from your contacts)
2. **Write a script** - What Clara should say
3. **Set the timing** - When to start calling (respecting business hours)
4. **Clara calls** - She reaches out to each customer, one by one
5. **Log results** - Appointments booked, messages left, objections handled
6. **Review analytics** - See what worked and what didn't

**Result: 10-15 hours of calling work, automated in 30 minutes of setup.**

## Key Features

**Intelligent Dialing**
- Clara recognizes voicemail and leaves messages
- She handles objections ("I'm not interested" → "I understand...")
- She books appointments automatically

**Smart Timing**
- Respects business hours (doesn't call at midnight)
- Spreads calls throughout the day (doesn't call 1,000 people at 9am)
- Timezone-aware (calls during their business hours, not yours)

**Compliance**
- TCPA-compliant (hits Do Not Call list, respects opt-outs)
- Call recording and consent handling
- Detailed audit trail for compliance

**Analytics**
- See exactly how many calls were made
- How many answered, how many voicemails
- How many appointments booked
- Listen to any call recording

## Quick Start

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to Outbound">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound](https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound)**
</Step>

<Step title="Create Campaign">
Click **"Create New Campaign"**
</Step>

<Step title="Upload Contacts">
- **Option A:** Upload a CSV file (phone, name, context)
- **Option B:** Select contacts from your existing database

The system will validate phone numbers.
</Step>

<Step title="Write Your Script">
Tell Clara what to say. Keep it natural:

> "Hi [Name], this is Clara calling on behalf of [Business]. I'm reaching out because we have a new service you might be interested in. Do you have a quick minute?"

**Pro tip:** Write like you're having a real conversation, not reading a script.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Timing">
- **When to start:** Today, tomorrow, or schedule for later
- **Call window:** 9am-5pm, 10am-8pm, etc.
- **Timezone:** Your timezone or customer's timezone
- **Rate:** Spread calls (don't overwhelm people)

Example: Start Monday, call between 9am-5pm, one call per minute.
</Step>

<Step title="Review & Launch">
Preview your script, check your contact list, and click **"Launch Campaign"**

Clara starts calling immediately (or at the scheduled time).
</Step>

</Steps>

## Campaign Types

### Appointment Reminders

"Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Business]. I'm calling to remind you of your appointment tomorrow at [time]. Press 1 to confirm, 2 to reschedule."

✅ **Best for:** Reducing no-shows

### Lead Follow-Up

"Hi [Name], this is Clara. We met last week and I promised to follow up about [topic]. Are you still interested?"

✅ **Best for:** Keeping leads warm

### Survey/NPS

"Hi [Name], quick question: How would you rate your recent visit? Press 1-5."

✅ **Best for:** Getting feedback quickly

### Event Promotion

"Hi [Name], you're invited to [event]. It's [date] at [location]. Want to register? Press 1 for yes."

✅ **Best for:** Driving attendance

## Viewing Results

After launching, watch your campaign dashboard update in real-time:
- **Calls Made** - How many numbers Clara has reached
- **Answered** - Live conversations
- **Voicemail** - Left messages
- **Appointments Booked** - From successful calls
- **Conversion Rate** - % of calls that converted

Click any call to see:
- **Full transcript** - What was said
- **Recording** - Listen to the call
- **Outcome** - What happened (booked, voicemail, declined, etc.)

## Managing Active Campaigns

While a campaign is running, you can:
- **Pause** - Stop calling (resume later)
- **Stop** - End the campaign
- **Adjust rate** - Speed up or slow down calling
- **Add contacts** - Upload more numbers mid-campaign

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound](https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound)** > Your Campaign > **Settings**.

## Compliance & Legal

<Warning>
Outbound calling has legal requirements. We handle most of it, but you need:
- ✅ Right to call these people (they're existing customers or opted in)
- ✅ Calling time compliance (respects Do Not Call, business hours)
- ✅ Recording consent (Clara mentions "This call may be recorded")
</Warning>

Read [Compliance](/outbound/compliance) for full details.
