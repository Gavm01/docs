## Add Your First Client

Follow these steps to onboard a new client onto AgentClara.

<Steps>

<Step title="Create a Sub-Account">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/master/accounts](https://agentclara.app/master/accounts)** (you need agency owner permissions).

Click **"Create New Sub-Account"**

**Fill in:**
- **Client Name** - Their business name
- **Industry** - What they do (salon, gym, etc.)
- **Email Contact** - Who manages this client for you

Click **Create**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Client's Agent">
You're now inside the client's account. You'll see their agent setup:

1. Go to **[https://agentclara.app/agent](https://agentclara.app/agent)**
2. Set up their Clara (personality, knowledge, voice)
3. Add their business hours
4. Connect their calendar

Do this for your client OR invite them to do it themselves.
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Their Channels">
The client needs to talk to customers through at least one channel (SMS, email, Instagram, etc).

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)** and connect:
- **SMS** (if they use text messages)
- **Email** (if customers email them)
- **Instagram/Facebook** (if they use social media)
- **WhatsApp** (if they want WhatsApp support)

You can do this, or send them a setup link to do it themselves.
</Step>

<Step title="Invite Their Team">
If the client has staff who should access their account:

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/settings/users](https://agentclara.app/settings/users)**

Click **"Invite User"**

**Fill in:**
- **Email** - Their work email
- **Role** - What they can do:
  - **Owner** - Can change billing and invite others (usually the client owner)
  - **Admin** - Can use all features but can't change billing
  - **User** - Can read conversations and reply but can't change configuration

Click **Send Invite**.

The person will get an email and can start using the platform immediately.
</Step>

<Step title="That's It">
Your client is now live and can start serving customers with Clara.

To come back and manage them later, use the **Sub-Account Selector** in the top left of your dashboard.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Managing Multiple Clients

Once you have several clients, you can:

**Switch Between Clients**
- Use the dropdown in the top left
- It shows all your sub-accounts

**See All Conversations**
- Go to **[https://agentclara.app/master/overview](https://agentclara.app/master/overview)**
- This shows activity across ALL your clients at once

**Check Usage & Billing**
- Go to **[https://agentclara.app/master/billing](https://agentclara.app/master/billing)**
- See what each client is using and what they're being charged

## Invite Format

When you invite someone to a sub-account, they get an email like:

> "You've been invited to join [Client Name] on AgentClara. Click here to activate your account."

They'll create a password and start using the platform. Simple.

## Can Clients Invite Their Own Team?

Yes. If you make someone an **Owner**, they can:
- Invite their own team members
- Change their agent configuration
- Manage their own billing (see their costs)

If you make them **Admin**, they can use everything except invite people or change billing.

## Permissions Matrix

| Action | Owner | Admin | User |
|--------|-------|-------|------|

| Reply to customers | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Configure agent | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I created a sub-account but now I can't get back to it">
  Use the **Sub-Account Selector** in the top left (it's a dropdown showing your client names). Click on the client you want.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I invited someone but they didn't get the email">
  1. Check their spam folder
  2. Make sure you used the right email
  3. Try inviting them again
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I want to remove a user from a sub-account">
  Go to **Settings** > **Users** in that sub-account, find the user, and click **Remove**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change someone's role after inviting them?">
  Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Users**, find them, and click **Edit** to change their role.
</Accordion>
