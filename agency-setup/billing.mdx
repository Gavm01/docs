## How AgentClara Billing Works

You pay based on what your clients **use**, not a fixed monthly fee.

**Key concept:** Clara's work is measured in **interactions**. An interaction is one customer message + one Clara response.

## What Counts as an Interaction?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Conversation" icon="chat">
    Customer sends message → Clara responds = 1 interaction
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voice Call" icon="phone">
    Customer calls → Clara answers = 1 interaction
  </Card>
  <Card title="Appointment Booking" icon="calendar">
    Customer schedules time → Clara confirms = 1 interaction
  </Card>
  <Card title="Follow-up" icon="bell">
    Clara sends automated reminder = 1 interaction
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**One message back-and-forth = 1 interaction.**

## Agency Billing Model

As an agency, you:
1. **Get billed by AgentClara** for total usage across all clients
2. **Charge your clients** whatever you want
3. **Keep the difference** as profit

**Example:**
- You have 3 clients
- Client A uses 500 interactions/month
- Client B uses 300 interactions/month
- Client C uses 1,200 interactions/month
- **Total: 2,000 interactions**

**Your cost:** 2,000 × $0.35 = **$700**

**What you can charge:**
- Client A: $250 (markup)
- Client B: $150 (markup)
- Client C: $600 (markup)
- **Your revenue: $1,000**
- **Your profit: $300**

You set the pricing for each client.

## Set Limits Per Client

To prevent surprises, you can set a **max interactions** for each client:

1. Go to **[https://agentclara.app/master/accounts](https://agentclara.app/master/accounts)**
2. Click on a client
3. Go to **Settings** > **Plan & Limits**
4. Set **Max Monthly Interactions**

If they hit the limit, they'll get a warning. They can request more, and you can approve it.

## Payment Methods

**How you pay us:**
- **Credit card** - Billed automatically each month
- **Bank transfer** - For enterprise customers (contact sales)
- **Invoice** - For large organizations

Go to **Settings** > **Billing** to update your payment method.

## Invoices & Receipts

At the end of each month, you'll get an invoice showing:
- Total interactions across all clients
- Total cost
- Line items (broken down by client if you have multiple)

Download it in **Settings** > **Invoices**.

## Overage Charges

If a client exceeds their plan limit:
- Clara keeps working
- The overage is charged at your plan rate
- You'll see it on next month's bill

**Example:** Client is on "1,000 interactions/month" plan, uses 1,500 interactions
- Base: 1,000 interactions = $350
- Overage: 500 interactions = $175
- Total: $525

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="How do I charge my clients?">
That's up to you. Common approaches:
- **Flat monthly fee** - "You pay $500/month for up to 2,000 interactions"
- **Per-interaction markup** - "You pay $0.75 per interaction (we pay $0.35)"
- **Tiered** - Different prices for different volume levels

Most agencies use a flat monthly fee so clients have predictable costs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if a client doesn't use their full allotment?">
Unused interactions don't carry over. Each month resets. This encourages clients to use Clara—they've already paid for it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change a client's plan?">
Yes. Go to **Master** > **Accounts**, find the client, and click **Edit Plan**. Choose a new plan. The change applies next billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if a client wants to pause for a month?">
You can pause their account temporarily. Go to **Master** > **Accounts**, find them, and click **Pause**. No charges while paused.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I get on a higher tier?">
Contact sales@agentclara.ai and tell them your expected volume. They'll set you up with the right plan and pricing.
</Accordion>
