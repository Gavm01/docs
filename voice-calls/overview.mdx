## Clara on the Phone

Clara isn't just text-based. Customers can call your business and talk to Clara directly. She can:
- **Answer calls instantly** (no more waiting on hold)
- **Answer questions** about hours, pricing, services
- **Book appointments** by collecting dates and times
- **Take messages** for human staff to follow up
- **Handle call routing** (transfer to a real person if needed)

## How It Works

**When a customer calls your business number:**

1. Clara answers (she sounds like a real person)
2. She listens to what they need
3. She answers questions or books an appointment
4. If needed, she transfers the call to a staff member

**You get a notification** of missed calls, messages, and scheduled appointments—all in your [https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats) inbox.

## Getting Started with Voice

To set up voice calls:

<Steps>

<Step title="Get a Phone Number">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations/phone-numbers](https://agentclara.app/integrations/phone-numbers)**

Choose:
- **Get a new number** - We'll give you a local number (costs ~$2-5/month)
- **Use your existing number** - Redirect your current business number to Clara (requires carrier setup)

Click **Get Number** and follow the steps.
</Step>

<Step title="Teach Clara About Calls">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/agent](https://agentclara.app/agent)** > **Voice Personality**

Tell Clara:
- How to greet callers ("Hi, thanks for calling Beautiful Cuts!")
- What information to collect
- When to transfer calls to a human

Add this to your system prompt:
> "You're Clara, our phone assistant. Greet callers warmly. If you can't help, ask for their phone number and say we'll call them back."
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Your Calendar">
If Clara is booking appointments, she needs to see when you're available.

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)** > **Calendar**

Connect Google Calendar, Outlook, or Calendly.
</Step>

<Step title="Test It">
Call your new phone number from your cell phone. Clara should answer. Try:
- "What are your hours?"
- "Can I book an appointment?"
- "Do you offer [service]?"

If she answers wrong, go back and add information to her knowledge base.
</Step>

</Steps>

## View Call Records

All calls show up in your **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)** inbox.

Each call shows:
- **Who called** (their phone number)
- **When** (date and time)
- **Duration** (how long the call lasted)
- **Transcript** (what was said)
- **Outcome** (appointment booked, message left, etc.)

Click a call to see the full conversation and take action.
