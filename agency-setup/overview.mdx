## Run an Agency on AgentClara

If you're managing customer service for multiple clients (or planning to), AgentClara lets you:
- **Manage all clients from one dashboard**
- **White-label Clara for your clients** (they think it's your product)
- **Track usage and billing separately** for each client
- **Scale without adding staff**

## How It Works

You create **Sub-Accounts** (one for each client). Each sub-account has:
- Its own inbox
- Its own agent configuration
- Its own billing
- Its own users and permissions

Think of it like managing separate businesses from one place.

## Your Role: Agency Owner

As the agency owner, you can:
- ✅ Create new sub-accounts (new clients)
- ✅ Invite users to specific clients
- ✅ Set permission levels (who can do what)
- ✅ See all conversations, contacts, and analytics across clients
- ✅ Manage billing for each client
- ✅ White-label the platform with your branding

## Key Concepts

**Sub-Account**: A separate workspace for one of your clients. It has its own conversations, agents, and billing.

**Role**: Determines what someone can do:
- **Owner** - Full control, can invite users and change billing
- **Admin** - Can use all features but can't change billing or invite users
- **User** - Can read conversations but can't change agent config

**Sub-Account Selector**: In the top left of [https://agentclara.app](https://agentclara.app), you'll see a dropdown. Use it to switch between your clients.

## Multi-Client Inbox

All your clients' conversations show up in one inbox. You can:
- Filter by client
- Tag conversations
- Escalate urgent issues
- Route to specific team members

This means you can manage 10 clients (or 100) without 10 separate logins.
