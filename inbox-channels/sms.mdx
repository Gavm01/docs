## How Clara Handles SMS

Clara can respond to texts with:
- **Business hours** - "We're open Mon-Fri 9am-5pm"
- **Service info** - "Haircuts are $45, includes wash and style"
- **Appointment booking** - "We have 3pm or 5pm available. Which works?"
- **Confirmations** - "Your appointment is confirmed for Saturday at 3pm"
- **Reminders** - "Reminder: you have an appointment tomorrow at 2pm"

**Example SMS conversation:**

Customer: "Hi, can I book an appointment?"

Clara: "Sure! We have availability Friday at 2pm or Saturday at 11am. Which works?"

Customer: "Saturday 11am"

Clara: "Perfect! What's your name?"

Customer: "John"

Clara: "Great John! Saturday 11am is confirmed. We'll text you a reminder Friday. See you then!"

✅ **Appointment booked. Calendar updated. Reminder scheduled automatically.**

## SMS Guidelines

### Message Length

- **1 SMS = 160 characters** (includes spaces)
- **Longer = costs more** (171 chars = 2 SMS, counted as 2 messages)

**Clara's tip:** Keep messages short. Texts should be quick replies, not essays.

### Timing

- **Send during business hours** (9am-5pm in customer's timezone)
- **Don't text after midnight**
- **Avoid weekends unless time-sensitive** (appointment reminders OK)

### Compliance

<Warning>
You must comply with TCPA and local laws:
- ✅ Get customer opt-in before texting
- ✅ Include "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- ✅ Honor opt-out requests immediately
- ✅ Don't send marketing texts to people who didn't consent
</Warning>

Clara handles most of this automatically, but you're responsible for opt-in.

## Sending Links via SMS

You can include links in SMS:

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I got a number but customers' texts aren't coming through">
  1. Check the number is active (**Phone Numbers** page should show "Active")
  2. Make sure Clara's system prompt is complete (she needs knowledge to respond)
  3. Text the number yourself to test
  4. Refresh the inbox page

  If still broken, contact support@agentclara.ai
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clara's replies are too slow">
  SMS responses are usually instant (under 5 seconds). If they're slow:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Clara might be processing (complex questions take longer)
  3. The carrier might be delaying (rare but happens)

  Retry with a simpler question.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My SMS costs are too high">
  Check:
  1. Are you sending very long messages? (Each 160 chars = 1 SMS)
  2. Are you broadcasting to too many people?
  3. Is Clara responding to spam/bot messages?

  Ways to reduce:
  - Keep messages under 160 characters
  - Only broadcast to real customers
  - Set Clara to not respond to certain keywords
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use my existing business number?">
  Maybe. If it's a real phone number (not a landline), you can:
  1. Port it to AgentClara (rare, requires carrier coordination)
  2. Or get a new SMS number from us and keep your current number for calls

  Most people choose option 2 (easier).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do customers have to opt-in?">
  Yes, legally in most countries:
  - **Marketing texts** - Need explicit opt-in
  - **Transactional texts** (confirmations, reminders) - Generally don't
  - **Opt-out** - Must honor STOP requests immediately

  When a customer texts you first, that counts as opting in.
</Accordion>
