## Meet Clara

Your AI-powered customer service assistant that never sleeps. Clara handles conversations across every channel—SMS, email, Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, calls—all from one inbox.

While you sleep, Clara is:
- ✅ Answering customer questions
- ✅ Booking appointments
- ✅ Collecting information
- ✅ Following up with customers
- ✅ Learning from interactions

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="How much does Clara cost?">
  We charge per interaction (each message sent/received). No hidden fees.

  Typical costs:
  - SMS: ~$0.01 per message
  - Voice calls: ~$0.15 per call
  - Outbound: ~$0.15 per call

  See [Billing](/agency-setup/billing) for details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Clara really AI or just a chatbot?">
  Clara is powered by Claude, an advanced AI. She understands context, handles complex requests, and learns your business. Not a simple scripted bot.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I try Clara for free?">
  Yes! 30-day free trial. No credit card needed. You get everything included.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if Clara can't help a customer?">
  Customers can ask to speak to a human. You get notified. You take over the conversation. Seamless escalation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use Clara for my agency and white-label it?">
  Yes! Create sub-accounts for each client. White-label with your logo and colors. Charge clients however you want. See [Agency Setup](/agency-setup/overview).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Clara work with my calendar?">
  Yes. She syncs with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Calendly. When customers book through Clara, it appears on your calendar automatically.
</Accordion>
