## Meet Clara

Clara is an AI-powered assistant that answers your customers' questions, books appointments, and manages conversations across every channel where your customers reach you—all without you typing a single response.

Think of Clara as your always-on customer service team. She learns your business, knows your policies, and handles the work that would normally take hours of your time each day.

## What Clara Does

**Answers Questions**: Clara reads your knowledge base and responds to customer inquiries in seconds. No more waiting in your inbox.

**Books Appointments**: Customers can book time directly through Clara, and she syncs everything with your calendar automatically.

**Manages Every Channel**: SMS, email, Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, voice calls—Clara handles them all from one unified inbox.

**Works Around the Clock**: While you sleep, Clara is still responding to customers, handling follow-ups, and collecting information.

**Learns Your Business**: Feed Clara your documents, FAQs, and templates. She uses them to give accurate answers that sound like they came from your team.
