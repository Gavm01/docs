## Automatic Voicemail Transcription

When someone calls and you don't answer, Clara can take a voicemail and automatically transcribe it. No more listening to a 3-minute message when you could read it in 5 seconds.

## How It Works

1. **Customer calls** your business number
2. **You/Clara don't answer** (you're busy or it's after hours)
3. **Clara's voicemail greeting plays** (customizable)
4. **Customer leaves a message**
5. **Message is recorded and transcribed**
6. **You get a notification** with the full text

## Set Up Voicemail Greeting

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/agent](https://agentclara.app/agent)** > **Voicemail** and write what callers hear:

**Good example:**
> "Hi, thanks for calling [Your Business]. We're not available right now, but we'll call you back within 24 hours. Please leave your name, number, and a brief message."

**Keep it under 15 seconds.**

Clara will automatically record what they say after your greeting.

## Find Voicemail Messages

All voicemail shows up in your main inbox: **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)**

Look for:
- **From:** Customer's phone number
- **Type:** "Voicemail" (with a voicemail icon)
- **Transcript:** What they said (text below the message)

Click on any voicemail to see:
- Full transcript
- Original audio (play button)
- Timestamp
- Customer's number

## Read the Transcript

The transcription is usually accurate, but not perfect. Listen to the audio if:
- Something doesn't make sense
- You need to catch the exact tone
- The customer spoke very fast or with an accent

Click the **play button** to hear the original message.

## Respond to Voicemail

Once you read the transcript, you can:

**Option 1: Call Them Back**
- Copy their number from the voicemail
- Call them directly

**Option 2: Send an SMS**
- Click "Reply"
- Send a text message back
- This shows in the same conversation thread

**Option 3: Add a Note**
- Click the message
- Add internal notes for your team

## Set Hours for Voicemail

By default, voicemail is available 24/7. You can change this:

Go to **Settings** > **Business Hours** and set when voicemail is active.

**Common setup:**
- **Business hours:** Clara answers
- **After hours:** Voicemail available

## Voicemail Best Practices

<Check>
**Keep the greeting short.** Callers don't want to listen for 30 seconds.
</Check>

<Check>
**Be specific about response time.** "We'll call you back within 2 hours" is better than "as soon as possible."
</Check>

<Check>
**Give clear instructions.** "Please leave your name, number, and reason for calling" helps you get the info you need.
</Check>

<Check>
**Update your greeting seasonally.** "We're closed for the holidays" or "We're very busy this week" sets expectations.
</Check>

<Check>
**Check voicemails daily.** Don't let messages pile up—respond within your promised timeframe.
</Check>

<Warning>
**Don't make the greeting a sales pitch.** Keep it professional and brief.
</Warning>

<Warning>
**Don't forget to follow up.** If someone left voicemail, they're waiting for a response.
</Warning>

## Transcription Accuracy

Our transcription is 90-95% accurate, but errors can happen:
- If the customer spoke very fast
- If there's background noise
- If they have a heavy accent
- If they speak a mix of languages

**If the transcript is wrong:**
1. Listen to the audio
2. Understand what they actually said
3. Reply accordingly
4. (Errors help us improve over time)

## Common Issues

<Accordion title="I'm not getting voicemail notifications">
  Check:
  1. **Settings** > **Notifications** — voicemail alerts enabled?
  2. Check your email spam folder
  3. Make sure you're logged in to the dashboard

  If still not working, email support@agentclara.ai
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The transcript is very inaccurate">
  Listen to the audio (click the play button) to understand what was really said.

  Transcription isn't perfect, but the recording is. If accuracy is critical, listen to the original.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I want callers to choose an option (press 1 for sales, press 2 for support)">
  That's called "Interactive Voice Response" (IVR). Email support@agentclara.ai and ask about our Advanced Call Routing feature.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I disable voicemail and route calls to my cell phone instead?">
  Yes. Go to **Agent** > **Escalation** and set your cell phone number. All calls go directly to you.

  (Voicemail will still be available as a backup if you don't answer.)
</Accordion>
