## Call Templates

Instead of writing scripts from scratch, start with templates. Modify them for your business, then launch.

Go to **[https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound](https://agentclara.app/launch/outbound)** > **Templates** tab.

## Using a Template

<Steps>

<Step title="Browse Templates">
See available templates:
- Appointment Reminders
- Lead Follow-Up
- Win-Back Campaigns
- Survey/NPS
- Upsell/Cross-Sell
- And more
</Step>

<Step title="Read the Script">
Click a template to see the full script. It's a good starting point.
</Step>

<Step title="Customize for Your Business">
Change:
- Business name
- Specific services/products
- Your tone and style
- Details (appointment times, offers, etc.)

**Keep the structure** but make it yours.
</Step>

<Step title="Save Your Version">
Click **"Save as Custom"** to save your modified version.

Next time you create a campaign, you can use your custom template—no need to rewrite it.
</Step>

<Step title="Use in Campaign">
When creating a campaign, under **Script**, select your template and it auto-fills.

Edit if needed, then launch.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Built-In Templates

### 1. Appointment Reminder

**When to use:** Reduce no-shows

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Business]. I'm calling to remind you of your appointment tomorrow at [Time]. We're looking forward to seeing you! Press 1 to confirm or press 2 if you need to reschedule."

**Customizations:**
- Change appointment time variable
- Add "We're here to help" if applicable
- Mention any special notes (bring ID, arrive early, etc.)

### 2. Lead Follow-Up

**When to use:** Keep warm leads engaged

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Business]. We connected last week about [Topic]. I wanted to follow up and see if you've had a chance to think about it. Do you have 5 minutes to chat?"

**Customizations:**
- Mention specific discussion/product
- Add urgency if applicable ("limited time offer")
- Adjust for email/meeting/demo

### 3. Win-Back Campaign

**When to use:** Re-engage lapsed customers

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Business]. We haven't seen you in a while and we miss you! We've added [New Thing], and I'd love to get you back in. Are you interested in a [Offer]?"

**Customizations:**
- Mention what's new (products, services, features)
- Make the offer specific (discount, free trial, etc.)
- Add personal touch ("Remember when you loved...")

### 4. Survey / NPS

**When to use:** Get feedback quickly

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], quick question: How would you rate your recent experience with us? Press 1 for poor, 5 for excellent."

**Customizations:**
- Ask specific question ("How was your service?")
- Add follow-up ("If 4 or 5, why?" / "If below 3, what could we improve?")
- Use sentiment to route (book follow-up for low ratings)

### 5. Event Invitation

**When to use:** Drive attendance

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], you're invited to [Event] on [Date] at [Time]. It's free and you'll learn about [Topic]. Want to register? Press 1 for yes, 2 for maybe later."

**Customizations:**
- Add event details
- Mention who should attend ("Salon owners only")
- Include registration link or instructions

### 6. Product Launch

**When to use:** Announce new offerings

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Business]. We just launched [Product], and I think you'd love it. It [Benefit]. Want to hear more? Press 1 for yes."

**Customizations:**
- Describe what's new
- Explain the benefit
- Make it sound exciting

### 7. Upsell / Cross-Sell

**When to use:** Increase order value

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], thanks for your recent purchase of [Product]. You might also love [Related Product]. It pairs perfectly. Want to learn more? Press 1 for yes."

**Customizations:**
- Reference their actual purchase
- Suggest complementary product
- Include special bundle pricing if applicable

### 8. Re-Engagement

**When to use:** Bring back inactive subscribers

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], we noticed you haven't opened our emails in a while. We'd love to stay in touch! Are you still interested in our updates? Press 1 to stay subscribed, 2 to unsubscribe."

**Customizations:**
- Reference their previous engagement
- Offer value ("We're sharing industry secrets next week")
- Make unsubscribe easy (required by law)

## Creating Custom Templates

### Save Your Script as a Template

After you write a script for a campaign:

1. Go to **Templates** tab
2. Click **"Create New Template"**
3. Enter:
   - **Template name** - "Holiday Promotion 2024"
   - **Description** - "For customers who haven't bought in 6 months"
   - **Script** - Your full script text
   - **Tags** - "seasonal", "promotion", "win-back"

4. Click **Save**

Now you can reuse this script without rewriting it.

### Organize Templates

You can organize templates by:
- **Type** - Reminder, follow-up, survey, etc.
- **Audience** - VIP, lapsed, new
- **Season** - Holiday, summer, etc.
- **Campaign** - By business type

Use tags to make them easy to find.

## Template Variables

Use these in any template to auto-fill contact data:

| Variable | Example |
|----------|---------|
| `[Name]` | John |
| `[FirstName]` | John |
| `[LastName]` | Smith |
| `[Company]` | Acme Corp |
| `[Email]` | john@acme.com |
| `[Phone]` | 555-1234 |
| `[CustomField1]` | Any custom data you upload |
| `[Date]` | March 15 |
| `[Time]` | 2:00 PM |

**In your script:**
> "Hi [FirstName], this is Clara calling about your [CustomField1]..."

If a field is missing, Clara says "that information" instead.

## Best Practices

<Check>
**Start with a template.** It's faster than writing from scratch.
</Check>

<Check>
**Customize it.** Generic scripts convert worse. Make it personal.
</Check>

<Check>
**Save your version.** Once you customize it, save it as a custom template for reuse.
</Check>

<Check>
**Test first.** Call yourself and listen to how it sounds. Adjust if needed.
</Check>

<Check>
**Keep it short.** Under 30 seconds is ideal. Longer = lower conversion.
</Check>

<Warning>
**Don't use corporate jargon.** People on the phone appreciate casual, friendly language.
</Warning>

## Template Performance

Metrics show you which templates work best:

Go to **Reports** and filter by template:
- **Conversion rate** - % who said yes
- **Appointment booked** - Direct bookings
- **Voicemail left** - For follow-up
- **Call length** - How long people stayed on

Use this to identify your best-performing scripts. Copy them and refine them further.

## Examples by Industry

### Salon / Spa

"Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Salon]. It's been a while and we'd love to see you! We have availability Friday at 3pm or Saturday at 11am. Want to book?"

"Hi [Name], thanks for joining us! We have new equipment and classes starting next month. When would be good to give you a tour? Press 1 for Monday, 2 for Wednesday."

### Clinic / Medical

"Hi [Name], we're calling to confirm your appointment next Tuesday at [Time]. Please press 1 to confirm, 2 to reschedule."

### E-commerce

"Hi [Name], you ordered [Product] last month. We hope you love it! We have something similar you might enjoy—want to hear about it? Press 1 for yes."

### Professional Services

"Hi [Name], this is Clara from [Company]. We worked with [Similar Client] and saw a 40% improvement in [Metric]. Are you open to a quick conversation? Press 1 for yes."
