## Get Clara Running

The fastest way to get Clara talking to your customers. Follow these steps in order—it takes about 15 minutes.

<Steps>

<Step title="Sign Up">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app](https://agentclara.app)** and create your account.

Use your work email so your team can find you later.
</Step>

<Step title="Start the Setup Wizard">
After you sign up, you'll see a big **"Setup Wizard"** button. Click it.

(If you don't see it, go to your dashboard and look for the wand icon ✨ in the top right.)
</Step>

<Step title="Tell Us About Your Business">
The wizard will ask:
- What industry are you in? (salon, gym, clinic, etc.)
- What's your business name?
- What time do you usually work?

Just be honest. You can change this later.
</Step>

<Step title="Add Your First Channel">
Pick how customers reach you:
- **Text messages (SMS)**
- **Email**
- **Instagram**
- **WhatsApp**
- **Facebook Messenger**
- **Website chat**

Pick at least one. The wizard will walk you through connecting it.
</Step>

<Step title="Customize Your Agent">
Give Clara a personality:
- What's her name? (Hint: Clara is our favorite 😊)
- What's her tone? (Professional, friendly, casual)
- What should she know about your business?

This is where you teach Clara about your products, policies, or services.
</Step>

<Step title="Test It Out">
At the end of the wizard, you'll see a chat box. Try it:
- "What are your hours?"
- "Can I book an appointment?"
- "Do you have availability Tuesday?"

If Clara answers correctly, you're ready. If not, you'll have a chance to teach her more in the next step.
</Step>

<Step title="You're Done!">
Click **"Finish"** and you're live. Clara is now responding to real customers.

Your inbox is at **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)** - you'll see conversations come in as customers contact you.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Next: Teach Clara About Your Business

Now that Clara is live, she needs more information to answer questions correctly.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Add Documents" icon="file-text">
    Upload PDFs, policies, or guides so Clara can reference them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Write FAQs" icon="question">
    List common questions and how you want Clara to answer them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create Templates" icon="edit">
    Write sample responses so Clara knows your tone and style.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Go to **Settings** > **Knowledge** to add these.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I completed the wizard but Clara isn't responding">
  Check that you actually connected your channel (SMS, email, etc). If you skipped that step:

  1. Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)**
  2. Click on your channel (SMS, Instagram, etc)
  3. Follow the setup steps to connect it
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clara is responding but giving wrong answers">
  She needs to learn. Go to **Settings** > **Knowledge** and add:
  - Your business hours
  - Common questions (FAQ)
  - Your policies (pricing, cancellation, etc)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I add more channels later?">
  Yes. Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)** and add as many as you want.
</Accordion>
