## White-Label AgentClara

White-labeling means your clients think they're using **your** product, not ours. They see your logo, your colors, your branding everywhere.

This is great for:
- **Resellers** - Sell AgentClara as your own service
- **Agencies** - Keep your brand consistent
- **Enterprise Clients** - Internal tools with your company colors

## How to Set It Up

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to Master Account Settings">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/master/accounts](https://agentclara.app/master/accounts)** and look for **White-Label Settings**.

(This only shows if you're an agency owner.)
</Step>

<Step title="Upload Your Logo">
Click **Upload Logo** and pick your company logo (PNG or SVG).

This appears in the top left and in emails.
</Step>

<Step title="Pick Your Colors">
- **Primary Color** - Your main brand color (appears in buttons, links, highlights)
- **Accent Color** - Secondary color (hover states, highlights)
- **Dark Mode** - Dark version of your colors for night mode

If you don't know your brand colors, ask your designer or marketer.
</Step>

<Step title="Update Company Name">
Change "AgentClara" to your company name. This appears:
- In the header
- In emails
- In settings pages
- In support links
</Step>

<Step title="Set Support Email">
When customers or clients need help, they should email **you**, not us.

Enter your support email:
- `support@yourcompany.com`
- `help@yourcompany.com`
- Etc.

This email appears in error messages, help links, and support buttons.
</Step>

<Step title="(Optional) Custom Domain">
This is for advanced agencies. You can run AgentClara on your own domain:

Instead of `agentclara.app`, your clients access `yourcompany.com`

**How:**
1. Contact support@agentclara.ai
2. We'll set up a custom domain for you
3. Your clients see only your branding

(This requires a paid plan.)
</Step>

<Step title="Save and Test">
Click **Save**. Your branding now appears across the platform.

Open an incognito window and test it to see what your clients see.
</Step>

</Steps>

## What Your Clients See

When a client logs in, they'll see:
- ✅ Your logo in the top left
- ✅ Your company colors everywhere
- ✅ Your support email in help sections
- ✅ Your company name in the header
- ✅ Links to your documentation (not ours)

They won't see anything about "AgentClara."

## Per-Client Branding (Advanced)

If you want **different branding for each client**, you can set it:

1. Switch to a specific sub-account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Branding**
3. Set their specific logo, colors, and name
4. Save

That client sees their own branding, not the agency branding.

## Email Templates

When Clara sends appointment reminders or follow-ups, they come with your branding:
- Your company name in the greeting
- Your support email in the footer
- Your colors (optional)

You can customize email templates in **Settings** > **Templates**.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="My logo looks blurry on the dashboard">
  Use a high-resolution logo (at least 200x200 pixels). SVG files work best.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The colors look different on dark mode">
  You can set different colors for light and dark mode. In White-Label Settings, toggle "Dark Mode" and set the colors separately.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I want to use my domain but don't know how">
  Email support@agentclara.ai and mention "custom domain." We'll walk you through it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can each of my clients have their own branding?">
  Yes! Switch to a sub-account and go to **Settings** > **Branding**. You can set branding per client.
</Accordion>
