## Connect Instagram & Facebook

Customers reach you through Instagram DMs and Facebook Messenger. Connect both so Clara can respond directly from your AgentClara inbox.

## Requirements

You need:
- **An Instagram business account** (or creator account with messaging enabled)
- **A Facebook page** linked to your business
- **Admin access** to the account/page
- **Message approval** enabled in account settings

<Info>
If you only have a personal Instagram account, you'll need to switch to a business account first. It's free and takes 2 minutes in the Instagram app.
</Info>

## How to Connect

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to Integrations">
Go to **[https://agentclara.app/integrations](https://agentclara.app/integrations)**
</Step>

<Step title="Find Instagram & Facebook">
Look for **"Instagram" or "Facebook"** and click **Connect**
</Step>

<Step title="Log Into Your Meta Account">
You'll be redirected to Facebook/Meta. Log in with your account.

(This handles both Instagram and Facebook since Meta owns both.)
</Step>

<Step title="Grant Permissions">
Meta will ask if AgentClara can:
- ✅ Read your messages
- ✅ Send messages
- ✅ View your contacts
- ✅ Manage your page

Click **Approve**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Your Account">
- **For Instagram:** Choose your business account
- **For Facebook:** Choose your page

You can connect both—they'll show as separate channels in your inbox.
</Step>

<Step title="Back to AgentClara">
You'll be redirected back to AgentClara. It will say **"Connected"**.

Messages will start appearing within 5 minutes.
</Step>

<Step title="Test It">
Send yourself a DM:
1. From your personal account, DM your business page/account
2. See it appear in AgentClara (labeled "Instagram" or "Facebook")
3. Reply from AgentClara
4. See your reply on Instagram/Facebook

Done!
</Step>

</Steps>

## Instagram DMs in Your Inbox

Once connected, DMs appear in **[https://agentclara.app/chats](https://agentclara.app/chats)**:

- Filter by **Channel: Instagram** to see only Instagram messages
- Filter by **Channel: Facebook** to see only Facebook messages
- Click any conversation to see the thread
- Type a reply and send (it appears in their DMs instantly)

## How Clara Handles Instagram/Facebook

Clara can respond to DMs with:
- **Product info** - "That shirt comes in blue and green"
- **Appointment booking** - "We have availability Friday at 2pm"
- **Questions** - "What size do you need?"
- **Order status** - "Your order ships tomorrow"
- **Escalation** - "Let me connect you with our sales team"

**Example conversation:**

Customer DM: "Do you have this in a larger size?"

Clara: "Great question! We have XL, XXL, and XXXL available. Which fits best?"

Customer: "XL, how much to ship?"

Clara: "Shipping is free on orders over $50. That size is in stock. Want me to send you the link?"

Customer: "Yes please"

Clara: [Sends product link]

**All in DMs. No need to leave Instagram.**

## Instagram Features

### Story Replies

When someone replies to your Instagram Story, Clara can respond:
- "Thanks for commenting!"
- "DM us to learn more"
- "Link in bio for details"

### DM Stickers & Quick Replies

Instagram allows:
- **Stickers** - Add personality to responses
- **GIFs** - Make responses more engaging
- **Quick replies** - Save common responses and use them with one click

Set these up in **Settings** > **Quick Replies**.

### Automations

You can set up automations:
- **Auto-reply for welcome messages** - "Thanks for DM! We respond within 2 hours"
- **Appointment confirmations** - "You're booked for Saturday at 3pm"
- **Order updates** - "Your order shipped today!"

## Facebook Messenger Features

### Chat with Customers

Facebook Messenger is similar to Instagram but can handle:
- **Longer conversations** - Messenger works better for detailed discussions
- **Payments** - Some Facebook pages accept payments through Messenger
- **Attachments** - Customers can send receipts, screenshots, etc.

### Broadcast Notifications

You can send notifications to customers (with opt-in):
- Business hours changes
- New products
- Limited-time offers
- Event announcements

Only send to customers who subscribed.

## Responding to Messages

**All responses go through Clara** unless you take over:

1. Clara automatically replies to DMs
2. If Clara can't help, the message is marked for human review
3. A team member takes over and replies manually
4. Conversation continues in the same thread

**Customers never see "this is an AI" unless you tell them.**

## Managing Multiple Accounts

You can connect:
- **Multiple Instagram accounts** (if you have multiple brands)
- **Multiple Facebook pages** (if you manage pages for different clients)

Each shows as a separate channel in your inbox. Switch between them using the **Channel Filter**.

## Conversation Management

In Instagram/Facebook conversations, you can:
- **Tag** people or issues
- **Assign** to team members
- **Snooze** to be reminded later
- **Archive** when resolved
- **Mark as spam** if needed

All from AgentClara—don't need to open Instagram app.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I'm not seeing DMs in AgentClara">
  1. Check your connection (**Integrations** > **Instagram/Facebook** should say "Connected")
  2. Send yourself a test DM
  3. Refresh the page
  4. Wait 5 minutes (new messages take time to sync)

  If still not working, disconnect and reconnect.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clara's replies aren't showing on Instagram">
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh Instagram (sometimes there's a delay)
  3. The message might be pending (check timestamp)

  Look for error message in AgentClara (usually says why the message failed).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I have multiple Instagram accounts—which one is connected?">
  Go to **Integrations** > **Instagram** and see which account is shown.

  To switch accounts:
  1. Disconnect
  2. Reconnect and choose a different account

  You can connect multiple accounts if you add them one by one.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I manage a client's Instagram from AgentClara?">
  Yes! If they give you admin access to their Instagram page:
  1. You log into their Meta account
  2. Grant permission to AgentClara
  3. Their messages appear in your inbox

  You can manage multiple clients' Instagram accounts in one dashboard.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to respond on Instagram app too?">
  No! Everything happens in AgentClara. Your team doesn't need Instagram app access.

  (Of course, you can still use the Instagram app if you want—messages sync both ways.)
</Accordion>
